Into the Simulation | Ethan Yoshioka | March. 26th, 2020

Is Minecraft proof we live in a simulation?

Minecraft has blown up over the last few decades, and many are left wondering what's next for simulation games. We've gone as far as to create a 1:1 exact replica of the entire Earth in Minecraft... Imagine what kind of simulations we could accomplish centuries from now. Is it possible we could accomplish a simulation as complex as life?

Minecraft has over 500 million players. That's a lot of players. :)
